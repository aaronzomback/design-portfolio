Aaron is a product designer and web developer based in Lyon, France.

His work combines a background in science and filmmaking, with technology and user-centered design thinking. Previously, he worked as a Sr. Research Associate at a biotech research lab in Brooklyn, NY.

When COVID-19 forced confinement, Aaron was inspired to learn how to build things. He began learning how to code in order to develop web applications. Now he appreciates the insights that writing code has provided to his design approach.

His current interests are virtual/augmented reality, science education, healthcare, biotech, media, poetry, bitcoin, and chess.
